ABOUT US

Robotics Society is a platform where students from various branches work together to nurture their technical understanding and culture their innovative ideas and dreams about robotics into reality . Students take up challenges from day to day life scenarios, orient the challenges to feasible solution form, stimulate the thought process of students and convert their ideas to prototypes by their technical skills, and creativity.
-Aware the students about the role of robots in industries and day-to-day life.
-Provide a platform for robotic enthusiasts to collaborate and exchange ideas
-Mentor the potential robotic enthusiasts to take part in research activities
-Facilitate students to learn robotics & create robots using their creative skills.
